Privacy Policy

Fast Grader is currently in an early stage of development. As such, more analytics tools will be incorporated into the platform, and your use of the site will be analyzed in detail for the development of the project. Additionally, this means that we reserve the right to change this policy at any time without notice.

Fast Grader will not sell your data to third parties. We will not sell email lists to advertisers. However, we will utilize Google AdSense to support free use of our platform with advertisements. Please note the following regarding Google AdSense:

Google AdSense

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

LogRocket

LogRocket will be used to analyze your usage of the grading app and continuously improve it. Most of the content on the page, including grades, comments, and student submission content is captured by this tool. However, we have ensured that LogRocket will not capture any student names whatsoever, so any assignment submission data it does have visibility of will be anonymous.

Additionally, the following third-party platforms will receive information about your usage of this website, as is the norm on nearly all websites:

  • Google AdSense
  • Google Analytics

Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule ("COPPA")

This website is COPPA compliant, because any personal information about individuals under 13 years of age is provided by an adult who has the permission to do so. Any student information in our database is treated as sensitive data under the presumption that all students are potentially under thirteen years of age. Student names and grading data is protected by industry best practice security.

Account Deletion

Because this site is in an early stage of development, if you would like delete your account, please send me a personal email at jackdevriesmusic@gmail.com so I can accommodate that request.
