Privacy Policy

Fast Grader is currently in an early stage of development. As such, more analytics tools will be incorporated into the platform, and your use of the site will be analyzed in detail for the development of the project.

In particular, LogRocket will be used to analyze your usage of the grading app and continuously improve it. Most of the content on the page, including grades, comments, and student submission content is captured by this tool. However, we have ensured that LogRocket will not capture any student names whatsoever, so any assignment submission data it does have visibility of will be anonymous.

COPPA

This website is COPPA compliant, because any personal information about individuals under 13 years of age is provided by an adult who has the permission to do so. The only personal information of students that this service collects and stores are names. Student names are never sent to third party services, but are stored in our database.

Account Deletion

Because this site is in an early stage of development, if you would like delete your account, please send me a personal email at jackdevriesmusic@gmail.com so I can accomidate that request.
