LogRocket

LogRocket will be used to analyze your usage of the grading app and continuously improve it. Most of the content on the page, including grades, comments, and student submission content is captured by this tool. However, we have ensured that LogRocket will not capture any student names whatsoever, so any assignment submission data it does have visibility of will be anonymous.

Additionally, we will likely utilize these third party services on our site in the near future, although they are not implemented presently.

  • Google AdSense
  • Google Analytics

Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule ("COPPA")

This website is COPPA compliant, because any personal information about individuals under 13 years of age is provided by an adult who has the permission to do so. Any student information in our database (as defined under COPPA) is treated as sensitive data under the presumption that all students are potentially under thirteen years of age. Fast Grader uses industry standard security practices, including Transport Layer Security (TLS), and user authentication via Google.
