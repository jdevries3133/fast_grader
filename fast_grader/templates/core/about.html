About Us

This project was made by me, Jack DeVries. I am a teacher from New Jersey, and this site is a solution to the pain and slowness involved in taking a quick look at student work in Google Classroom, and getting feedback to students.

I wanted to find a better way to efficiently get my students work in front of my eyes, and to get my feedback in front of theirs. As a hobbyist developer, I recognized that a keyboard-first tool and text-focused tool would help me reach that goal, and I hope that you will find it useful in your own grading workflow, too.

Do you have any ideas or feature requests for this site? I would love to hear what you think towards the continued growth of this project! Open up an issue on GitHub, or send me a personal email at jackdevriesmusic@gmail.com!
