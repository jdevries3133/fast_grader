Help

Where do comments and grades go?

Since the fast grader is currently in an early stage of development, your comments and grades are simply saved, and accessible to you in your profile home page. Comments and grades do not make the round trip back into Google Classroom currently, although after we ensure that teachers like using the grading tool, the next objective will be to develop that feature, so that importing comments and grades back into google classroom is a seamless process.

How do I report a bug?

Bug reports are extremely appreciated! Click the button above to submit a bug report on GitHub. Or, send me a personal email at jackdevriesmusic@gmail.com.

How do I request a new feature?

Use the same process as submitting a bug report! Open up an issue on the GitHub repository, or send me an email!
