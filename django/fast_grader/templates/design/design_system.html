Color Demos

The round colors are the ones from the color pallate. Depending on the hue and saturation of the palate color, they live at different points along the tailwind gradient, so that all colors have even gradients from dark to light. If there are certain specific hues we want to focus in on, they can be given specific aliases, like "blue-main", or just "primary", etc.

The name for each color in tailwind config is in parenthesis aside the color name.
